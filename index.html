Our Mission

Located in Tel Aviv, Oz Fund is a path for investors to take part in groundbreaking Israeli technology. We specialize in sectors poised to redefine the future, including Artificial Intelligence, Cyber, Defense, Digital Heath, Biotech, and Unmanned Vehicles. OzFund is dedicated to providing returns, through funding technologies that make a different to the industries they play in.

  • Connecting Investors with Transformative Israeli Tech: We identify and invest in pre-funding, pre-seed, and seed-stage startups that exhibit the potential to revolutionize their industries.
  • Bridging Global Entities with Cutting-Edge Solutions: We facilitate partnerships between international companies and government agencies with Israel's most innovative technologies, allowing entrepreneurship into the most established industries.
  • Driving Business Development for Israeli Startups: We support Israeli companies by matching them with clients abroad. This validates their market fit, fosters growth and expands their global footprint.

Our Founders

Our criteria: founders who are doing something different. Product-Market fit is something we will determine, but ultimately it is Israeli founders ability to tread upstream that differentiates the Israeli tech ecosystem.

  • Technology Scouting: We aim to work with innovative founders with ambitious ideas. Our responsibility is to provide a return to our investors, so we will need to ensure your product has a market. This is something we can define with you.
  • Facilitating Investments: We will review your pitch deck and give critical feedback, in order to amplify your idea in front of investors. We will manage the deal flow process, with the aim to bring the funding that will launch your idea.
  • Business Development: Market traction is necessary to validate your business and gain feedback for future growth. Our team is relentless in finding target customers and building relationships that will bring early commercial success.
